Unit6 Section A 2a-2d (供读背用)

一、单词

weak  /wi:k/ adj. 虚弱的，柔弱的

god  /ɡɒd/ n. 上帝，神

remind  /rɪˈmaɪnd/ v. 提醒，使想起

bit  /bɪt/ n. 一点，小块

a little bit  有点儿，稍微

silly  /ˈsɪlɪ/ adj. & n. 愚昧的；傻子，蠢货

instead of  代替，反而

二、词组

1.继续做某事  continue to do sth.

2.被某人感动  be moved by sb.

3.派某人去做某事  send sb. to do sth.

4.拿走  take away

5.提醒某人...  remind sb. that

6.某人的故事 the story of sb.

7.做某事的好方法  a good way to do sth.

8.同意某人 agree with sb.

9.解决问题  solve a problem

10.代替做某事  instead of doing sth.

11.许多其他方法 many other ways

12.对某事有不同的观点 have different pinions about sth.

13.你们两个中没有一个人  neither of you

14.一个故事的许多面  many sides to a story

15.放弃做某事  give up doing sth.

16.有点傻 a little bit silly

三、句型

1.一天当愚公和他的孩子们正忙着移山时，一个人看到了他们。

A man saw Yu Gong and his children when they were working on moving the mountains.

2.这个人一说完话，愚公就说他的家人能在死后继续移山。

As soon as the man finished talking, Yu Gong said that his family could continue to move the mountains after he died.

3.这个故事提醒我们，你永远不会知道什么事情是可能的，除非你试图使它发生。

This story reminds us that you can never know what’s possible unless you try to make it happen.

4.移山似乎是不可能的。

It doesn’t seem very possible to move a mountain.

5. 这个故事努力向我们表明如果你努力工作，凡事皆有可能！愚公一直尽力并且没有放弃。

The story is trying to show us that anything is possible if you work hard! Yu Gong kept trying and didn’t give up.
